About

Posted on Nov 12, 2023

Hello! I’m Collin Heenan, a driven second-year undergrad specializing in Economics and Computer Science.

At the core of my academic pursuits is a deep engagement with Machine Learning and Quantitative Research. I’m particularly fascinated by the intricacies of Language Models, exploring how these sophisticated algorithms can process, understand, and generate human-like text.

My focus lies in the technical aspects of Machine Learning - from neural network architectures to algorithm optimization. I’m passionate about harnessing these technologies to develop more efficient and accurate models, especially those that can analyze and interpret large datasets with minimal human intervention.

In the realm of Economics, my interest is specifically angled towards leveraging computational methods to uncover insights within economic data, applying Machine Learning techniques to predict market trends, and exploring the potential of AI in economic forecasting.
